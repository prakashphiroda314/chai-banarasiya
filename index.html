Our Story

A Slice of Banaras in Jaipur

Chai Banarasiya is more than just a multicuisine restaurant — it's a warm celebration of Banaras' culture, traditional flavors, and vibrant ambiance.

Founded with the idea of blending heritage with hospitality, we invite you to enjoy every sip and story under our inviting roof. Our interiors are inspired by the soulful lanes of Varanasi — designed to evoke nostalgia, warmth, and joy.

From authentic Banarasi masalas and street-style flair to carefully crafted multicuisine dishes, every plate captures the true essence of Banaras right here in the heart of Jaipur.
